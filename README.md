## About

I turn rough ideas into usable tools: AI-assisted productivity systems, developer dashboards, web applications, and knowledge platforms. My recent work is moving toward practical AI products that can collect context, structure decisions, summarize work, and make specialized knowledge easier to use.

- Currently focusing on research evidence platforms, AI workflow automation, and full-stack prototypes.
- Comfortable moving across Python, Go, JavaScript, Java, Vue, HTML, and CSS.
- I prefer projects that are small enough to ship, but structured enough to keep evolving.
